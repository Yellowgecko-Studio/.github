<h1 align="center">Yellowgecko Studio</h1>

<strong>Yellowgecko Studio</strong> is an indie video game studio based in Spain, focused on creating unique gameplay experiences through unexpected combinations of genres, mechanics, and ideas.

We believe that games should feel fresh, memorable, and deeply immersive. Our goal is to build worlds that invite players to explore, imagine, and connect with something larger than a single story.

At the heart of our work is an expanding shared universe, <strong>The Timeless Universe</strong>: a mysterious setting where all of our games take place, each revealing a different side of the same world.

## Current Projects

</h3>

**The Timeless Bastion** is a post-apocalyptic metroidvania with demanding combat inspired by platform fighters.

Set in a decaying world shaped by forgotten conflicts and ancient mysteries, the game challenges players to master movement, timing, and combat precision while uncovering the secrets hidden within its vast interconnected environments.

The game combines exploration, intense encounters, and a deep narrative foundation within **The Timeless Universe**.

### The Timeless Delivery

**The Timeless Delivery** is a lighthearted platformer about letting your imagination fly in order to deliver a package to its recipient.

Playful, creative, and full of unexpected situations, the game explores a different tone within the same universe as *The Timeless Bastion*. It focuses on freedom, creativity, and the joy of finding unusual ways to overcome obstacles.

## The Timeless Universe

Both *The Timeless Bastion* and *The Timeless Delivery* are part of the same expanding franchise.

Although each game offers a different style, tone, and gameplay experience, they are connected through a larger world filled with history, mystery, and hidden links waiting to be discovered.

Our long-term goal is to continue expanding this universe through new games, stories, and experiences, allowing players to see the same world from many different perspectives.
